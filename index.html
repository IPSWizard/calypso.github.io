About CALYPSO 2k22

"IN HOC SIGNO in this sign we conquer", is the emblem of this prestegious institution, "Madras Christian College Higher Secondary School" founded in the year 1835, unfazed by the arduous journey of the world. This esteemed institution has sent forth scores of men and women who have climbed the ladder of success in all challenges of life. Adding to it all, the school focuses on the holistic development of students from various socio-economic backgrounds.

At this juncture, we the students have planned to organize "CALYPSO 2K22" an Inter School annual cultural event to provide an opportunity for the students to showcase their talents and interests in extracurricular activities. Calypso 2022 is a music based cultural event which encourages and helps honing the student's creativity, gain vast experience, analyze and evaluate outcomes and discover their personal aptitude.
